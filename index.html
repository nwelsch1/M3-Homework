Visit Lake Como and Stay at the beautiful Villa Senagra

Looking for a great place to vacation this summer? Join us in Lake Como Italy for an experience of a lifetime.

  • 7 bedrooms
  • 3 bathrooms
  • Sleeps 13

Beautiful villa in a superb location. A few minutes walk to the centre of Menaggio with great restaurants and shops plus a nearby Lido with lakeside beach. The villa is clean, comfortable and well equipped. Large bedrooms, bathrooms and magnificent furniture. There is an outside pool and hot tub and well kept garden. There is a beautiful large dining room and additional outside seating so the whole family can eat together outside on the balcony. Walking distance to the boats to Varenna, Bellagio, Como and many other lakeside towns.
